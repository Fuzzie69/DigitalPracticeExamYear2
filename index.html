Welcome to the Year 2 Practice Exam

This exam is a consolidated practice for Level 2 and Level 3 electrical trainees in New Zealand.

You will have 1.5 hours to answer 60 questions.

This is a timed practice exam intended to test knowledge across a broad range of topics (not open book).

Covered subjects include (representative):

  • Mathematics for Electrical Trades
  • Atomic Theory & Electrochemistry
  • Resistance, Circuits & AC/DC Theory
  • Motors, Transformers & Generation
  • Safety, Isolation, Testing & Legislation
  • Power Supplies, Lighting, Earthing & Protection
  • Soldering, Components, Practical (EAS-Q / EST)
  • National Grid, Switchboards & Fault Finding
